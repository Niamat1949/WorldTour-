FAQs

When do lakes start to freeze ?

Typically, lakes in the Canadian Rockies start to freeze in late November, and they stay like that until the end of winter. The ice on the lakes can start to melt as early as late April, but most years we need to wait until June to be able to swim in them. The exact timings vary each year depending on several weather conditions and cannot be guaranteed.

Is it cold in the Rockies?

Weather in the Rockies during the winter months can get very cold. It is strongly recommended to pack warm clothes, thermal and waterproof layers, a winter coat, snow or warm boots, gloves and a hat to ensure you have the best time on your winter adventure!

Can i go skiing/snowboarding on this tours ?

While skiing/snowboarding is not included in the itinerary of this tour, you can add on a full-day lift ticket & equipment rentals to ski/board at Lake Louise Mountain Resort (additional cost, see optional add-ons below). We recommend this activity for skiers with an intermediate or advanced level, as beginner lessons are not available.
